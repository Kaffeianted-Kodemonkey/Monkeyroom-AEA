Our mentorship program is thoughtfully designed to provide you with valuable real-world experience and the opportunity to enhance your resume.

During the program, you will have the opportunity to work on client projects or company projects, based on skill set and project availability.

We believe this mentorship experience will be enriching and empowering for you as you develop your skills and contribute to the accessibility development community.

Mentoring through this program is non-paying, as our focus is empowering A11y Developers to become the best versions of themselves and land that dream job.

Thank you for joining us on this journey towards making a positive difference!

Let's Begin

To ensure smooth progress and efficient collaboration, all work will be tracked in HubSpot and GitHub.

For the evluation testing of all sites, we use the following assistive technologies and tools:

  • ANDI a free open-source bookmarklet designed to help test web content for accessibility.
  • Color Contrast Analyzer (CCA) an open-source tool displaying the contrast ratio for two selected colors. CCA is available at the following links:
    • CCA 2.5 for Windows
    • CCA 2.4 for macOS
  • WAVE Web accessibility evaluation tool
  • Screen reader: NVDA, Jaws
  • Mobile Devises: iSO, Andriod
  • Acrobat: CommonLook PDF Plug-in - Optional
